COLLABORATION

Collaboration with Ocean Cleanup Inc.

Our company, NEER ROBOTICS, is proud to collaborate with Ocean Cleanup Inc., a leading organization dedicated to removing plastic waste from the world's oceans. Together, we will develop innovative robotic solutions for ocean cleanup, making significant strides towards a cleaner and healthier marine environment.

Impactful Partnership

Through our partnership with Ocean Cleanup Inc., we will deploy robotic technologies in various ocean cleanup initiatives, removing tons of plastic waste and debris from ocean waters. Our joint efforts will garner international recognition and inspire others to join the fight against marine pollution.

Looking Towards the Future

As we continue our collaboration with Ocean Cleanup Inc., we remain committed to advancing technology-driven solutions for ocean conservation. Together, we envision a future where our oceans are free from plastic pollution, allowing marine ecosystems to thrive for generations to come.
