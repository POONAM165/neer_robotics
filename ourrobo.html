JELLYFISH ROBOT

Modeled after the graceful and efficient movement of jellyfish, the Jellyfish Robot features a dome-shaped body with tentacles extending downward.

Utilizes a pulsating motion similar to that of jellyfish for propulsion, allowing for smooth and energy-efficient movement through water.

Sensors: Equipped with advanced sensors to detect pollutants, microplastics, and other debris in the water, enabling targeted cleanup efforts.

Filtration System: Incorporates a filtration system within its body to capture and retain debris, preventing it from re-entering the marine environment.

SPIDER ROBOT

The Spider Robot draws inspiration from the anatomy and movement of spiders, featuring multiple legs arranged in a radial pattern around its body.

Equipped with multiple legs that enable it to traverse various terrains, including rugged landscapes and uneven surfaces, with agility and stability.

Each leg of the Spider Robot is equipped with articulated joints, allowing for flexible movement and adaptation to obstacles and terrain irregularities

Utilizes advanced navigation systems, such as GPS and obstacle detection sensors, to autonomously navigate through its environment while avoiding obstacles.

SHARK ROBOT

Modeled after the sleek and powerful appearance of sharks, the Shark Robot features a streamlined body with fins and a tail for efficient movement through water.

Designed with a hydrodynamic shape to minimize drag and maximize speed and maneuverability, allowing it to navigate through water with ease.

Equipped with a range of sensors, including sonar and cameras, to detect and track underwater targets, such as marine debris and pollution, as well as monitor environmental parameters.

OCTOPUS ROBOT

Inspired by the flexible and versatile nature of octopuses, the Octopus Robot features multiple tentacles extending from a central body, allowing for agile movement and manipulation.

Each tentacle is equipped with articulated joints and suction cups, enabling the robot to grasp and manipulate objects with precision and flexibility.

Utilizes a combination of jet propulsion and tentacle movement for locomotion, allowing it to navigate through water with agility and efficiency.

Equipped with sensors, such as cameras and sonar, to detect and identify objects in its environment, including marine debris and pollutants.
