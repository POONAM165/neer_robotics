OUR MISSION

At Neer Robotics, our mission is to preserve and protect our oceans. We are dedicated to reducing marine pollution, restoring marine ecosystems, and promoting sustainable practices for a healthier planet.

OUR ROBOTS

Shark Robot

A sleek, hydrodynamic marvel, mimicking the apex predator's agility, designed for efficient ocean cleaning and environmental monitoring.

Octopus Robot

A versatile, multi-limbed innovation, mimicking the cephalopod's intelligence and dexterity, designed for underwater exploration and intricate tasks.

Spider Robot

An agile, eight-legged marvel, inspired by arachnids' mobility, adept at navigating diverse terrains for search, rescue, and surveillance.

Jellyfish Robot

A graceful, translucent creature, pulsating through the water, with stinging tentacles, serving as both predator and prey in marine ecosystems.

OUR VISION

Vision of the NEER ROBOTICS is to make the oceans and rivers clean and free of plastic all over the world.
