NEW INNOVATIONS

Dolphin Scanner Robot

The Dolphin Scanner Robot utilizes echolocation technology similar to dolphins to detect underwater debris and obstacles. Equipped with small robotic arms, it can retrieve the debris and bring it to the surface for collection.

Krill Robot

The Krill Robot filters seawater through its appendages, capturing microplastics and other pollutants. The collected debris is stored in a detachable chamber for easy removal and disposal.

Manta Ray Skimmer Robot

The Manta Ray Skimmer Robot features a wide wingspan to skim the ocean surface, collecting floating debris in a mesh net attached to its underside. It can cover large areas efficiently and contribute to ocean cleanup efforts.
